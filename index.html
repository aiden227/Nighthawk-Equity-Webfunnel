Frequently Asked Questions

Three key differentiators: verified track record through third-party auditor, conservative underwriting that protects capital first, and principals who invest their own money in every deal. Most syndicators can't claim any of these.

Minimum investment is typically $50,000 per deal, with most investors participating at $100,000-$500,000 levels. Investment capacity varies by specific offering.

We target 15-16% IRR with 1.85x-2.0x equity multiple over 5-7 years. Quarterly distributions typically range 7-9% annually. Our conservative projections are designed to be exceeded, not missed.

These are illiquid investments with 3-7 year hold periods. Capital is returned through refinancing or sale. This illiquidity is part of what generates superior returns compared to public markets.

Depreciation deductions can shelter passive income and potentially reduce taxes on active income. Cost segregation studies often generate $15K-$25K in first-year deductions on $100K investment.

Our track record is verified by Verivest, an independent third-party auditor who confirms our background, managed capital, and performance history. We provide references from current investors and detailed deal documentation.
